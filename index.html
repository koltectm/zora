The Artist

Music born from the dark

Zara Nova emerged from the underground Los Angeles music scene with a debut EP that sent shockwaves through the alt-pop world. Her voice — equal parts haunting and hypnotic — pulls listeners into worlds where vulnerability becomes armour.

"I make music for the 3 am moments. For when the world gets too loud and the only thing that makes sense is sound."

With three critically acclaimed studio albums, sold-out world tours, and a Grammy nomination for Best Alternative Album, Zara has become one of the most compelling voices of her generation. Her latest record, Meridian, is her most cinematic work yet.
